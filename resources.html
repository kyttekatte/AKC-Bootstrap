Where are we located?
Near Edmonton, Alberta
When do our kittens go home?
We adopt out between 5.5 and 6 months old, no sooner. We allow our kittens to mature physically before alter surgeries.
Will we make an exception on when our kittens are adopted out?
No.
Why do we wait so long to adopt them out?
My vet and my own personal preferences. Anesthesia is very hard on tiny kittens. Also, being neutered too young can cause urinary health issues in males.
What do our kittens/cats come with?
Kittens/cats are sent home spayed/neutered, tattooes, up-to-date on all shots, dewormed and given a clean bill of health. Also flea and ear-mite treatments if needed.
Do we allow cross border adoptions?
We don't allow cross border adoptions, nothing against our US followers. We have a strict return policy and so cats need to be within reasonable distance.
